The city's charming piazzas, such as Piazza Navona and Piazza di Spagna, are perfect for leisurely strolls and people-watching. Vatican City, an independent city-state within Rome, is home to St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel, featuring Michelangelo's famous frescoes.
